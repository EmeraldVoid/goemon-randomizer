Ancient Cave Starring Goemon is a randomizer for Mystical Ninja Starring Goemon on the Nintendo 64, created by abyssonym. It allows you to shuffle door connections, change the starting characters from Goemon/Ebisumaru to Sasuke/Yae, and even begin with different items, offering a fresh and unpredictable gameplay experience.

This Website contains the seeds I have generated and attempted to run. Mystical Ninja Starring Goemon is one of my favorite N64 games, and this challenge has been a fun way to rediscover its world in new and unexpected ways.

If you're a fan of Mystical Ninja Starring Goemon and enjoy randomizers, this is a fantastic way to experience the game in a new light!

Don't forget to check out the latest seeds and try them for yourself. For up-to-date information and everything related to this project, visit the GitHub repository. You can also watch me run some seeds live on my Twitch channel.
